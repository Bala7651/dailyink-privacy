Privacy Policy for DailyInk

Last updated: 2026-02-27

Overview

DailyInk is a private, local-first diary app. Your diary entries, images, and settings are stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. DailyInk does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

All data (diary entries, attached images, and app settings) is stored locally in your device's storage using SQLite and the app's document directory. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

Permissions

DailyInk requests access to your photo library and camera only when you choose to attach images to a diary entry. These images are copied to the app's local storage; the app does not upload or share them.
